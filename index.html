System Documentation

Task 1: Compare Fuel Efficiency Across Manufacturers

Objective: Identify which manufacturers produce the most fuel-efficient vehicles
  1. Use the manufacturer dropdown to select specific brands
  2. Click "Sort A-Z" to alphabetize manufacturers
  3. Click "Sort by MPG" to rank manufacturers by fuel efficiency
  4. Hover over bars to see exact MPG values
  5. Click any bar to filter other visualizations to that manufacturer
Example Analysis: Sort by MPG to identify the most efficient manufacturers, then click on the top manufacturer to see their vehicles in the other views.

Task 2: Analyze Power vs Efficiency Trade-offs

Objective: Understand the relationship between engine power (horsepower) and fuel efficiency (MPG)
  1. Brush over points in the scatter plot to select vehicles
  2. Selected vehicles will highlight across all views
  3. Use the origin filter to compare different regions
  4. Note the color coding: American (blue), European (orange), Japanese (green)
  5. Click individual points to focus on specific vehicles
Example Analysis: Brush over high-MPG, high-horsepower points to find performance-efficient vehicles, then check their weight distribution.

Task 3: Examine Weight Distribution by Origin

Objective: Compare vehicle weight characteristics across different origins
  1. View the box plots showing weight distribution for each origin
  2. The box shows the interquartile range (25th to 75th percentile)
  3. The line inside the box represents the median
  4. Whiskers show the range (excluding outliers)
  5. Dots represent outlier vehicles
  6. Click on boxes or outliers to filter other views
Example Analysis: Compare American vs Japanese vehicles to see that Japanese cars tend to be lighter with less variation in weight.

Interaction Reference

Interaction Effect
Dropdown Filters Filters all visualizations simultaneously by selected criteria
Click Bar (Manufacturer) Filters all views to show only vehicles from that manufacturer
Brush in Scatter Plot Selects vehicles within the brushed area and highlights them across views
Click Point/Box Selects specific vehicle(s) and highlights across views
Reset Button Clears all filters and selections
